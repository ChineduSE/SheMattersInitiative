Our Story

About She Matters

We believe every teenage girl deserves to be seen, heard, and equipped — regardless of where she grows up.

Who We Are

A Movement, Not Just an Organisation

She Matters Initiative is a social impact movement focused on empowering teenage girls with clarity, confidence, leadership awareness, and menstrual hygiene education.

We go into schools and communities — meeting girls where they are — to spark conversations that many adults are too uncomfortable to have. We talk about periods. We talk about potential. We talk about power.

Our outreaches combine education, celebration, and community. We don't just lecture; we listen, we share, and we leave behind more than pads and pamphlets — we leave behind a shift in how girls see themselves.

Our Mission

To reach every teenage girl in underserved communities with the tools, knowledge, and inspiration she needs to rise — breaking the silence around menstrual health and unlocking her leadership potential.

🌍

Our Vision

A Nigeria where no girl misses school because of her period, and every teenage girl boldly steps into spaces of leadership and influence — knowing that she belongs there.

Why It Matters

The Problem We're Solving

Millions of Nigerian girls manage their periods with rags, foam, and other unsafe materials due to poverty and lack of education. Girls miss school. Some drop out permanently.

At the same time, girls are rarely seen in conversations about leadership, governance, or their own futures. Their voices are missing from rooms where decisions are made.

She Matters Initiative addresses both — because a girl who stays in school and knows her power is a girl who will change her community.
