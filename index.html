Our Community Projects

From menstrual hygiene awareness to leadership empowerment — here's how we've shown up for girls across Nigeria.

Ijebu Igbo, Ogun State

Menstrual Hygiene Awareness

Our very first outreach — we spoke about the dangers of using clothes and foam during menstrual flow and distributed sanitary pads to girls in need.

150+ Girls Enlightened
Oru Ijebu, Ogun State

Educational Outreach

We celebrated excellence by awarding the best 3 students in each class at Itamerin Junior and Senior Grammar School, and enlightened the entire student body.

300+ Girls Empowered
Ikpoba Hill, Benin City, Edo State

She Leads 2025

Held at Army Secondary School, this debate and enlightenment session awakened the love for leadership and politics in the hearts of girls, teaching them their rights.
